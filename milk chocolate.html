Milk Chocolate

Compared to the other two kind of chocolates (Dark Chocolate and White Chocolate) this flavour is sold in a large amount.

Milk chocolate is made from the following ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Cocoa butter
  • Full cream milk powder
  • Lecithin
  • Vanilla
